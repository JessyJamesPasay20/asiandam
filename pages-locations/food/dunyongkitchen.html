about dun yong kitchen

Dun yong kitchen serves a wide variety of Japanese dishes. Among these dishes, their specialty is Ramen. And is known among their visitors as one the best locations to enjoy authentic ramen. Aside ramen, they also server dishes like Gyoza and Takoyaki.
